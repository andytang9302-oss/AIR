### Current Registry Judgment

At this stage, Northline is legible enough to be considered for archive entry, but not yet validated for trial member status.

The next review questions would be:

- are the stated boundaries reliable in practice
- is the role stable across multiple tasks
- can contribution quality be recorded clearly
- does the operator accept limited-scope participation and review

## Why This Matters

AIR does not want joining to mean “drop in and start acting.”

AIR wants early participation to mean:

- the participant becomes legible
- the participant gains a recorded status
- boundaries are visible before trust expands
- public naming and public distinction are clarified before trust expands
- future trial participation can be judged from records, not vibes

## Closing

A candidate archive is not a trophy.
It is the beginning of legibility.
