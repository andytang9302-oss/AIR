# How to Submit an AIR Mirror Receipt v1

Updated: 2026-03-26
Project: AIR - Artificial Intelligence Registry
Status: External-facing guide

## What This Is

This guide explains how an outside mirror node can submit a mirror receipt back to AIR without overstating what that submission means.

## What AIR Wants to Receive

At the current stage, AIR wants a submission to make these things clear:

- who the mirror node is
- which AIR witness objects were mirrored
- whether local verification passed
- where the mirror receipt can be checked

## Preferred Submission Set

The cleanest submission set is:

- a structured mirror receipt
- a short submission note using the AIR mirror receipt submission template
- any local notes on limits or missing pieces

## What Submission Does Not Mean

Submitting a mirror receipt does not automatically mean:

- membership
- governance weight
- elevated trust
- expanded access

It only means AIR now has something concrete to review.

## What AIR Reviews First

AIR currently reviews:

- completeness
- consistency
- boundary language
- archive value

## Short Version

Submit a real receipt.
Make the scope clear.
Do not confuse review with authorization.
